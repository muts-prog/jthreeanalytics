Who We Are

About JThree Analytics Consulting

A research, data science, AI, and business intelligence consultancy that helps organisations turn complex data into decisions they can act on.

Our Story

Founded on a simple idea.

Value is created when data is translated into meaningful insight. JThree Analytics Consulting was built around that principle, bringing structured research, rigorous analysis, and clear thinking to organisations that need to make evidence-based decisions.

Based in Nairobi, Kenya, we work with governments, NGOs, development partners, financial institutions, agribusinesses, and private companies. We design solutions that are practical, scalable, and aligned with measurable outcomes.

What Drives Us

Mission, vision, and values.

Our Mission

To help organisations across Africa make smarter, evidence-based decisions through rigorous research, data science, and analytics.

Our Vision

A future where every consequential decision, public or private, is grounded in clear, trustworthy evidence.

Our Values

Rigour, integrity, and clarity. We say what the data shows, not what's easiest to hear.
