## Color Palette

| Token | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Navy Blue | `#0B1F3A` | Headings, dark sections (hero, footer, stats panel, CTA cards) |
| Blue | `#2563EB` | Buttons, links, active states |
| Blue Deep | `#1D4FC4` | Hover states |
| Teal | `#14B8A6` | Eyebrow labels, small highlights, icon accents |
| Light Grey | `#F8FAFC` | Alternating section backgrounds |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Primary background |

Defined once at the top of `style.css` under `:root` — change a value there and it updates everywhere.

## Typography

**Poppins**, used for both headings and body text, in a range of weights (400 body copy up to 800 for the boldest headlines). Loaded from Google Fonts in the `<head>` of every page.

## Voice & Tone

- **Evidence over adjectives.** Say what was done and how it helps, not how impressive it sounds.
- **Plain language.** No unexplained jargon — clarity is a stated value.

## Photography

Real business/research contexts — people reviewing data together, client meetings, genuine field/consumer settings — rather than generic stock cliché.
