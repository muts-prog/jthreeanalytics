Our Services

What We Do

Every engagement draws on the same structured methodology and rigorous analysis, tailored to organisations of every size, from a single research report to an ongoing analytics function.

Every service is built on the same structured methodology.

Market Research

Structured research that reveals customer behaviour, competitive dynamics, and market opportunity.

From consumer segmentation to competitor benchmarking, we design studies that answer the specific question your organisation is facing.

B2B Research

Understanding how businesses buy, decide, and grow, from procurement studies to partner mapping.

We interview and survey decision-makers directly, giving you a grounded view of how other businesses actually evaluate and buy.

Data Analytics

Data cleaning, statistical analysis, and clear reporting that answers the question you're asking.

We take messy, real-world data and turn it into a clean, reliable base for analysis. Then we answer the question that motivated the project in the first place.

Business Intelligence

Dashboards, KPI tracking, and reporting systems that turn raw data into clear insight.

Live dashboards give your leadership team one trusted view of performance, instead of five spreadsheets that don't agree with each other.

AI & Machine Learning

Predictive models and applied ML that forecast trends and give a forward-looking edge.

From demand forecasting to risk scoring, we build models that are explainable, so your team understands and trusts what's driving the prediction.

Monitoring & Evaluation

Tracking programme performance against indicators, so you know what's working and why.

We build M&E frameworks aligned with donor reporting standards, then run the ongoing data collection that keeps them current.

Agricultural Research

Farm-level and value-chain studies that support agribusiness and food-security decisions.

Fieldwork with smallholders and agribusinesses alike, mapping the value chain from farm gate to market.

Impact Evaluation

Rigorous evaluation designs that measure the real, attributable effect of a programme.

Baseline, midline, and endline designs, including comparison groups where appropriate, so results reflect the programme's true effect.

A research, data science, AI, and business intelligence consultancy helping organisations turn complex data into confident decisions.
